Final Blaze

Playing as a burning flame we call "Flint", you travel through a factory that's on the brink of breaking down. You are the source the factory depends apon, the energy it runs from. But you were place there against your will and it's time to escape!
In Final Blaze you navigate levels jumping over pitfalls, traversing platforms and avoiding certain death from the robots working there as well as the factory itself.

The Enemy AI

I worked on the enemy AI of this project, handling movement and interaction using a custom state machine.
